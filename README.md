# AI Marketing Ops Field Guide

Practical guides for building marketing dashboards, reporting workflows, and AI-assisted marketing automation.

This repository is a public field guide for marketers, analysts, founders, and builders who want cleaner systems around ads data. The focus is not folder structure for its own sake. The focus is repeatable workflows: what to check, what to build, what to avoid, and how to turn campaign data into useful decisions.

## What You Will Find

- Dashboard and reporting workflows for Google Ads, Meta Ads, and combined marketing data.
- Data QA checklists for catching bad numbers before they reach a client or decision maker.
- AI insight patterns that keep metric math deterministic and use language models only where they help.
- Templates and examples for practical reporting, automation, and review loops.

## Guiding Principles

- Make the workflow useful before making it fancy.
- Keep metric calculations outside AI prompts.
- Check data quality before writing insights.
- Prefer small, repeatable systems over one-off reporting scrambles.
- Write recommendations that a real operator can act on.

## Who This Is For

This guide is for people who work around performance marketing data and need practical operating systems, not generic marketing theory. It is especially useful if you are building:

- A Streamlit marketing dashboard.
- A monthly paid media report.
- A Telegram or Slack reporting bot.
- An AI-assisted campaign insight workflow.
- A lightweight reporting process for a small team or agency.
